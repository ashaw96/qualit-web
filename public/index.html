OUR WHY

The Construction Industry is suffering as a result of poor quality.

Low skills on all levels of the spectrum

The construction industry is facing a significant challenge due to a low-skilled workforce, marked by inadequate competence across trade, management, and design roles. This issue is compounded by a growing skills shortage, as fewer young people enter the sector while a large portion of the current workforce approaches retirement. The aging demographic not only reduces the availability of experienced workers but also highlights the lack of effective succession planning.
